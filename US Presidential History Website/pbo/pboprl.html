As a public figure and former President of the United States, Barack Obama's private life has been largely shielded from the media and the public. However, there are some details that are known about his personal life.
Barack Obama was born in Honolulu, Hawaii in 1961 to a Kenyan father and an American mother. He grew up in Hawaii and Indonesia before attending Occidental College in Los Angeles and transferring to Columbia University in New York City. It was during his time at Columbia that he began to develop an interest in politics and social justice.
In 1992, Barack Obama married Michelle Robinson, whom he had met while working at a law firm in Chicago. They have two daughters together, Malia and Sasha. The Obamas have been known to keep their family life private and away from the public eye.
In his spare time, Barack Obama enjoys playing basketball, reading, and listening to music. He has also been known to be a fan of TV shows such as "The Wire" and "Game of Thrones."
Since leaving the presidency in 2017, Barack Obama has continued to be involved in social and political issues through his foundation, the Obama Foundation. He has also written several books, including his memoir "A Promised Land," which provides insight into his personal and political life. Overall, while much of Barack Obama's private life remains private, he has shown a commitment to using his platform to promote social change and inspire others to do the same.
